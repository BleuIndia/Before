HEALTH INTELLIGENCE

Our Health Intelligence program comprises of two parts: assessment & intervention. The assessment will give you an overview of metabolites of energy function and metabolites produced by bacteria in your gut, it will also look at metabolic blocks and digestive abnormalities & test detoxification pathways - that often lead to micro-nutrient deficiencies, fixing this will lead to smooth gut-brain & metabolic functioning. At the next stage, is the intervention - where we correlate all your results into an intervention strategy that works for you. This strategy will allow you to rectify nutritional imbalances through targeted supplementation and nutrition that will enable your body & brain to perform at their optimal levels.

The program involves minimal effort from your end. A home collection kit will be dispatched to where ever you are, it will contain an assessment questionnaire & paraphernalia to collect a urinary sample (non-invasive). Rest assured, that it will not take more than 30 mins of your time.
